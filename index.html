Shinano Province, Japan - 1542

CHRONICLE I

SHOGUN DEFENSE

War spreads through the mountains of Shinano. Takeda Shingen, lord of Kai, advances into Suwa territory, where a coalition of rival clans gathers to halt his campaign.

Among them stands Suwa Yorishige, joined by warriors of the Ogasawara, Murakami, and other houses. Outnumbered but undeterred, the Takeda force prepares to strike through confusion, darkness, and rain.

Inspired by Kuniyoshi’s vision of the battle, smoke and fire sweep across the riverbank as arrows, shot, and burning debris surge toward the Takeda line.

You are the defender at the riverbank. Turn your guard toward incoming enemy attacks and deflect them before they break your position. Hold SPACE to widen your defensive stance when the assault grows too fierce.

Amid the chaos, allied messengers and supply bearers cross the field with vital provisions. Let them reach you to restore your strength, then endure the bombardment until victory is secured.
